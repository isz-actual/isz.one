Coins, Pins, and Patches

One studio for every piece your lodge needs, with consistent quality across the set.

Coins

Struck and die-cast coins in antique gold, silver, and copper, with hard or soft enamel, custom edges, and serial numbering on request.

Pins

Patches

Embroidered and woven patches for lodge events, riding clubs, and regalia. Merrowed or heat-seal borders to match your use.

Masonic Order Questions

Which bodies do you work with?

Blue Lodge, Scottish Rite, York Rite, Shrine, Order of the Eastern Star, Knights Templar, and the appendant and concordant bodies. If your group carries an emblem, we can render it.

What is the minimum order?

Fifty pieces per design. There are no mold fees and no setup fees, so the price you see covers the work.

Can you match our lodge emblem and degree work?

Yes. Send your artwork, a photo of a jewel, or a description. We produce a digital proof and refine it until you approve it, before anything goes into production.

How long does production take?

About four weeks after proof approval for a typical run. Tell us your installation or festive board date and we will plan the schedule around it.

Can we order coins, pins, and patches together?

Yes. Many lodges pair a coin with a matching lapel pin or patch. We keep the artwork consistent across the set.
