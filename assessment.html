Risk Scoring Methodology

Every vulnerability is scored using a structured risk matrix that combines likelihood and impact, weighted against existing control effectiveness.

Likelihood

How probable is exploitation of this vulnerability? Scored 1-5 from Rare to Almost Certain.

Impact

What is the consequence if exploited? Scored 1-5 from Negligible to Catastrophic.

Controls

How effective are current mitigations? Scored 1-5 from None to Fully Effective, reducing the inherent risk.

Risk Score = (Likelihood × Impact) − Control Effectiveness Offset

Critical (20-25) High (15-19) Medium (9-14) Low (4-8) Minimal (1-3)
