First Responder Order Questions

Who do you work with?

Police, sheriff, state patrol, corrections, fire departments, EMS, dispatch and 911 centers, transit police, and emergency management agencies.

Can you match our badge or department seal?

Yes. Send your artwork or a clear photo of an existing badge, patch, or seal. We produce a proof and refine it until you approve it.

What is the minimum order?

Fifty pieces per design. There are no mold fees and no setup fees.

Do you make memorial and retirement pieces?

Yes, including end-of-watch memorial coins, retirement coins, and award presentations with custom packaging.

How long does production take?

About four weeks after proof approval for a typical run. Tell us your event or academy date and we plan around it.
