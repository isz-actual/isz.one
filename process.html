How We Make Your Coins

From concept to completion, we guide you through every step of creating world-class challenge coins.

1

Request a Quote

Start by submitting your design concept, quantity, size, and timeline. Include any artwork, sketches, or descriptions of your vision. We review all requirements and provide a detailed quote within 24 hours.

2

Approval & Payment

Review the quote and invoice. Once you approve, we request payment to secure your project slot. We process quotes with MOQ of 50 units minimum, with production times of ~4 weeks for standard orders.

3

Design & Proof

Our team develops production-ready artwork from your specifications. We create a detailed proof showing the final design, finish options, and packaging. You review and approve—no hidden surprises.

4

Production

Once approved, your coins go into production at our precision manufacturing facility. We manage every detail: die creation, stamping, finishing, and quality control. Rush options available upon request.

5

Shipping & Delivery

Your finished coins are packaged and shipped to your location. We coordinate with your preferred carriers and can arrange international shipping with duties/tariffs handled by recipient.

What We Need From You

Design Elements

  • Artwork: Image, sketch, or detailed description
  • Text: All required wording with accurate spelling and dates
  • Size: Approximate dimensions you're targeting
  • Finish: Antique, polished, special coating

Logistics

  • Quantity: Number of coins needed
  • Timeline: Target delivery date and event date
  • Shipping: City/country for delivery
  • Budget: If applicable, target cost range
