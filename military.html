Military Order Questions

Which branches do you make coins for?

All of them. Army, Navy, Marine Corps, Air Force, Space Force, and Coast Guard, plus joint commands, schoolhouses, and veteran organizations.

Can you work from our unit crest or insignia?

Yes. Send your crest, command seal, or insignia and we produce a digital proof. We refine it until you approve it before production.

What is the minimum order?

Fifty pieces per design. There are no mold fees and no setup fees.

Do you make deployment, retirement, and change-of-command coins?

Yes, including serial numbering, custom edges, and presentation packaging for ceremonies.

How long does production take?

About four weeks after proof approval for a typical run. Give us your ceremony date and we plan around it.
