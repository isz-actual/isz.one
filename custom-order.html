Frequently Asked Questions

What's the minimum order?

50 coins per design. This allows us to create custom dies and ensure quality throughout production.

How long does production take?

Standard timeline is ~4 weeks for 50-250 coins. Rush options available upon request (may increase costs).

Do you charge mold fees?

No. All tooling and setup is included in the quote. You pay one price, we handle the rest.

Can you ship internationally?

Yes. We ship worldwide. Customers are responsible for duties and tariffs.

What if I have an urgent deadline?

Contact us directly at sales@isz.one to discuss rush options and availability.

Can you modify an existing design?

Absolutely. Send us the original design or photo and we'll work from there.
