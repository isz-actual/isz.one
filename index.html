About Ice Station Zebra

Founded in 2022 and based in Pittsburgh, PA, Ice Station Zebra LLC combines security consulting expertise with precision craftsmanship. We work directly with clients to turn concepts into finished coins — no middlemen, no templates.

From die design to final quality inspection, every step is managed in-house. Our clients include military commands, police departments, Masonic lodges, and Fortune 500 security teams.
