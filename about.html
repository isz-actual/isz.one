The Company

Founded January 3, 2022, Ice Station Zebra LLC brings together two decades of security consulting expertise with precision craftsmanship. We design and manufacture premium challenge coins for military units, government agencies, corporate organizations, and special projects.

p>

Based in Pittsburgh, PA, we serve clients who understand that challenge coins are more than merchandise—they're symbols of achievement, belonging, and organizational pride. Every coin represents a unique story and mission.
